# Nabella Studio — Product Context

## About

Nabella Studio is a beauty and wellness studio founded by Ruth Raldiris. The studio focuses on intentional, results-driven body care in a serene and personalized setting. The brand centers on empowering confidence, celebrating individuality, and enhancing natural beauty.

## Brand Voice

- Refined, warm, and professional
- Empowering without being clinical
- Speaks to women seeking confidence and self-care
- Avoids overly medical language; uses wellness-oriented phrasing

## Founder

Ruth Raldiris — body expert with a passion for sculpting and enhancing natural beauty. She is part of the brand identity and should be represented with care and elegance.
